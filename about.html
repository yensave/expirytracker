About FreshTrack

FreshTrack is designed to help users track the expiration dates of their purchased items, including food, groceries, cosmetics, and more. Many individuals overlook the expiration dates of their products, leading to unnecessary waste. Fresh Track aims to address this issue by providing an easy-to-use platform where users can monitor their items' expiration dates.

Additionally, the platform will send notifications or emails to alert users when a product is nearing its expiration date, encouraging timely usage. By doing so, Fresh Track helps to reduce food and product waste while promoting mindful consumption.
